1 - PURPOSE

Nipfune is an innovative app designed to tackle gender-based violence and boost women's journeys of overcoming it. It's a comprehensive app, packed with essential resources for safety, empowerment and recovery. With Features you can be a Lonely community. Nipfune provides a safe and supportive environment where inspiring stories are shared.
